# Full Description

**Unlock text from anywhere on the web!**

OCR Extension is a powerful, privacy-focused tool that lets you extract text from images, videos, PDF viewers, and protected websites where simple copy-pasting is blocked.

Unlike other tools that send your screenshots to a cloud server, this extension runs **Tesseract.js completely within your browser**. Your data stays on your device.
